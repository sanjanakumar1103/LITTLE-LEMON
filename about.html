About Us

Welcome to Little Lemon, your go-to Mediterranean restaurant in the heart of Chicago, Illinois. Our journey began when two Italian brothers, Mario and Adrian, shared a dream of bringing the flavors of the Mediterranean to the Windy City. With Mario's culinary expertise and family recipes, we've crafted a menu that celebrates the rich traditions of Italian, Greek, and Turkish cuisine. Our dishes are prepared with love and a modern twist, offering you a unique dining experience.

At Little Lemon, we believe in the power of food to bring people together. Our cozy and relaxed atmosphere welcomes you, whether you're joining us for a quick bite or a leisurely meal with friends and family. Thank you for being a part of our journey. We look forward to serving you and sharing the flavors of the Mediterranean with each delicious dish.
